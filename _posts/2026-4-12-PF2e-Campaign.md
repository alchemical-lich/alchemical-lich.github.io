**1 Desnan 4721**: The Hunting Grounds

The party pushes deeper into the hunting grounds and finds a Caligni hide-out. A difficult battle ensues that ends in a stand-off. The Caligni make an offer: if the heroes kill either a demon to the South or a dragon to the East, they will leave this cursed place.
