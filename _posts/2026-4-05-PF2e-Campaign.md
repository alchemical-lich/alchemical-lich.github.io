**1 Desnan 4721**: The Hunting Grounds

The party meets some Drow hunters. They challenge them to kill a giant spider in the larger cave. The party accepts. A difficult fight ends in hard-earned victory. The Drow respect strength and offer to guide the heroes to their leader—Quara Orshendiel. After some further exploration and rest, they take the Drow up on their offer and descend. 

They met the Drow leader and make a formal alliance. They Drow offer refuge and protection if the players pursue the destruction of Belcorra. First though, they need to find the three lenses of Nimbaloth and deal with the vampiric Urdefhan.
