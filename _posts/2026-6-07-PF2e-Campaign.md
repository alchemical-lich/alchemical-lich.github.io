**7 Desnan 4721**: The Hunting Grounds

The beginning of the end. The party makes their final preparations—decked out with new runes, potions, staffs, spell scrolls, and other trinkets, they complete their morning prayers, put on their armor, and equip their weapons. They approach the ominous stone double-doors leading to the Temple of Nhimbaloth. After a quick pause, they push through and encounter a giant skeleton, looming over them, signaling to approach and to follow it below into the temple proper. The come along and the trial of Nhimbaloth begins...
